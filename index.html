关于我们 / ABOUT US

LUXE BUILD LAYERS是一家专业的室内装修设计公司，致力于为客户打造独特、精致的居住和商业空间。我们的设计理念融合现代美学与实用功能，通过精心挑选的材料和独特的设计方案，为每个项目注入独特的个性与品味。

LUXE BUILD LAYERS is a professional interior design and renovation company dedicated to creating unique and refined living and commercial spaces. Our design philosophy combines modern aesthetics with practical functionality, infusing each project with distinctive personality and taste through carefully selected materials and unique design solutions.

凭借我们专业的设计团队和丰富的项目经验，我们能够为住宅、办公室、商业空间等不同类型的项目提供全方位的装修解决方案。从概念设计到施工管理，我们始终坚持高品质的服务标准，确保每个细节都完美呈现。

With our professional design team and rich project experience, we provide comprehensive renovation solutions for various types of projects including residential, office, and commercial spaces. From concept design to construction management, we maintain high-quality service standards to ensure perfect execution of every detail.
